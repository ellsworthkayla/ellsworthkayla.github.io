Josie Baby.

Josie is a long-hairred chihuahua and my pride-and-joy. I am basically obsessed with her. I adopted her from theAlachua County Humane Societythree years ago, and she has been my best friend ever since.


I started working withPhoenix Animal Rescue in January of 2013, and after realizing my knack for pet rescue, I decided it was time to share my beautiful baby with the world. I mean, I am in Public Relations after all. Isn't it my job to kickstart one's debut into the virtual world? -- Um, Yeah! So, there she was with her own blog, instagram and soon-to-come Twitter and Facebook! She mainly gets comments and "likes" from my mother, a.k.a "Grandma". But I am determined to make her go VIRAL!


The video you see here is a clip from her blog, Josie Baby. Josie uses her blog to express her thoughts on all things pet rescue. Whether that be showing her to support to local rescue groups and promoting their fundraisers, or voicing her opinion on prominent issues such as puppy mills, dog fighting or animal cruelty. She also shares cute little stories about herself and all the crazy, adorable, wierd things she does.
