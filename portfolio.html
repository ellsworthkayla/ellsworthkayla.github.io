Phoenix Animal Rescue

Please note that these materials were produced for the sake of my Public Relations Writing (PUR4100) course at UF and therefore, some of the information may have been made up for the sake of the assignment. My final project was to create a Media Kit for Phoenix Animal Rescue, the following are some of documents I included in that kit.


  • PAR Fact Sheet(PDF 150 KB) - The Who, What, When and Why of Phoenix Animal Rescue.
  • PAR Backgrounder(PDF 142 KB) - The history of the Phoenix Animal Rescue organization.
  • PAR Situation Analysis(PDF 44 KB)- Strength, Weaknesses, Opportunities and Threats facing Phoenix Animal Rescue.
  • PAR Biographical Sketch(PDF 292 KB)- A short biography on Michelle Dunlap, the founder and director of Phoenix Animal Rescue.
  • PAR Position Paper(PDF 88 KB)- Where Phoenix Animal Rescue stands on the issue of dog fighting.
  • Pit Bull Fact Sheet(PDF 150 KB)- Common myths associated with the Pit Bull breed and the real FACTS behind those myths.
  • PAR Media Pitch Letter(PDF 79 KB)-A letter to the media persuading them to cover a Pit Bull education seminar event.
  • PAR News Release(PDF 2.2 MB)- A News Release for PAR's "Education Meets Rescue" event.
  • PAR Letter to the Editor (PDF 55 KB)- A letter to the editor addressing the issue of animal cruelty.
  • PAR Feature Story(PDF 1.2 MB)- A story about an outstanding volunteer who got involved with pet rescue as a little girl.
  • PAR Newsletter Article(PDF 56 KB)-A 'summer safety tips' article for the Phoenix Animal Rescue newsletter
  • PAR Radio Public Service Announcements(PDF 71 KB)- Three PSA Radio Scripts regarding adoption, animal cruelty and the importance of spaying/neutering.
